Three research agents investigated whether the τ = 1 − F framework can go beyond FAPP (For All Practical Purposes) decoherence to provide **genuine** objective collapse, as required by Penrose's Objective Reduction program.

## Agent 1: Type III von Neumann Algebras

### Naive Argument (FAILS)
"QFT local algebras are Type III → no density matrices → no pure states → τ = 1 always"

Why it fails:
- Petz recovery map extends to Type III algebras (Faulkner et al. 2022)
- Gravity promotes Type III → Type II via Witten crossed product (2022)
- The algebra type actually gets BETTER with gravity, not worse

### Modified Argument (WORKS)
**Causal structure + infrared superselection** provides genuine irrecoverability:

1. **DSW mechanism** (Danielson-Satishchandran-Wald 2022-2025):
   - Superposition of masses radiates soft gravitons
   - Soft gravitons cross cosmological/event horizon
   - Information is genuinely lost to exterior observer
   - Not FAPP — causally impossible to recover

2. **Infrared superselection sectors**:
   - Soft graviton emission changes the superselection sector
   - Different sectors = algebraic barrier to recovery (not just practical)
   - This is genuine: no unitary on the exterior algebra can undo sector change
